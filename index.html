About Western Slope Willow

At Western Slope Willow, we specialize in growing premium willow and unique specialty plants in the heart of Olathe, Colorado.

With over 15 years of experience and a horticulture degree from Oklahoma State, our founder turned a lifelong dream into a thriving, family-run greenhouse.

We grow and ship all types of willow, including popular basketry willow, to customers across the country, from artists and gardeners to landscapers and nurseries. Everything is cultivated with care in our own 1600 sq ft greenhouse, using sustainable, hands-on growing practices.

What We Grow

From vibrant willows for basketry to unique plants for your garden, everything is grown with care.

Basketry & Landscaping Willow

We cultivate dozens of willow varieties, each with unique colors, textures, and properties. Whether you need strong, flexible rods for basketry and weaving, fast-growing trees for a living fence, or ornamental varieties for your landscape, we have the perfect cuttings for you. All are shipped dormant and ready to plant.

Unique Garden Plants

Beyond willow, we have a passion for growing unique and hard-to-find plants that add something special to your garden. Our selection changes seasonally but often includes heirloom flowers, rare herbs, and other botanical curiosities that you won't find at a typical nursery.
